Instructions

  1. Choose a drawer and pick a new Celonis product
  2. The time starts. You have 1 minute to draw and your team to guess the right product. If your team does, you win 2 points.
  3. If you guess correctly, you have the opportunity to win 1 extra point if you described the product correctly.
  4. If the time ends and your team did not guess the product, the other team have the opportunity to win the points.
