Preparation:

  • Choose a referee who will track the time and score. The referee will use the app following the blue text as a guideline.
  • Split into two teams, Team A and Team B, to compete in several rounds. Each round will have two turns, one for Team A to draw and another for Team B to draw.

Game:

  1. The first team will choose a person to draw. The referee will hand over the app to the person to pick a Celonis Product card to draw by clicking on the "pick a card" text.
  2. The referee will start the timer. The person has 1 minute to draw the product and the team to guess it right. If the team does, they will win 2 points.
  3. If the team guesses the product correctly, they will have the opportunity to win 1 extra point if they can describe the product and explain when to pitch it. The referee can check the description using the app.
  4. If the time ends and the team does not guess the product, the opposite team will have the opportunity to win the points.
  5. The referee will update the score and the turn will end. Now it is the turn of the other team.
  6. Play 4 or 5 rounds to find out who is the best team!
